IILM University, Gurugram

A private university known for management, liberal arts, and technology programs with strong industry exposure. Located in the corporate hub of Gurugram, it emphasizes internships, global immersion, and practical learning. Campus life is modern with active clubs and placement support.

IILM University Greater Noida

Focused on engineering, management, and applied sciences with a large, structured campus. It offers labs, project-based learning, and collaborations with industry partners. Known for a quieter academic environment compared to city campuses.

IILM Lodhi Road, New Delhi

One of the oldest IILM institutes, primarily known for management and PGDM programs. Situated in central Delhi, it provides strong corporate interaction and guest lectures from industry leaders. Smaller campus but academically focused.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Lucknow

A management-focused campus located in Gomti Nagar, offering BBA and MBA programs with industry exposure. Known for practical learning, internships, and placement-oriented training. Smaller campus but academically structured and career-focused.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Jaipur

Offers programs in management, design, law, and technology in a developing academic setting. The campus is relatively new and focuses on skill-based education and employability. Jaipur location gives access to both academic and cultural exposure.
